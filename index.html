The Big 3 in Tennis

Roger Federer


Roger is in my opinion the greatest tennis player who ever lived. He has a great serve and volley and his iconic one-hander which might be the best one-hander.



Novak Djokovic


Novak Djokovic is a great counter-striker who has some of the most consistent playing throughout his career. He never gives up until it's over



Rafael Nadal


Rafael Nadal is a unique player in that he is a left-handed player that allows him to play to his opponents natural weakpoint, but vice versa. Rafa is a left-handed player by choice; he is actually a navitvely right-handed player
